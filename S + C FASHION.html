Fashion

Thisrefers to the popular styles, trends, and designs in clothing, accessories, footwear, and even hairstyles that are embraced by society at a given time. It is a dynamic form of self-expression that reflects cultural, social, and personal identity. Fashion evolves constantly, influenced by designers, celebrities, subcultures, and historical events. It can range from haute couture (high-end fashion) to streetwear (casual and urban styles), and it often showcases creativity, craftsmanship, and individuality. Fashion allows people to express their personality and mood while keeping up with ever-changing trends.
