Jeans

Women

A Slim Jeans These jeans have a more tailored, close-fitting design throughout the legs, offering a sleek, modern look without being as tight as skinny jeans.

Bootcut Jeans Featuring a slight flare from the knee down, bootcut jeans are designed to be worn with boots, creating a balanced and flattering silhouette.

Jeggings A hybrid between jeans and leggings, jeggings are made from stretchy material that hugs the body for a sleek, form-fitting look, combining comfort and style.

Low-Rise Jeans With a waistband that sits below the hips, low-rise jeans offer a more casual, laid-back fit, popular in early 2000s fashion.

Wide-Leg Jeans These jeans have a loose, flowing fit from the waist down to the hem, offering a relaxed, stylish silhouette that gives a comfortable and trendy look.

Men

Loose Jeans These jeans have a relaxed, comfortable fit with extra room around the thighs and legs, offering a laid-back, casual look.

Ripped Jeans Featuring intentional tears or distressing, ripped jeans give off a rugged, edgy vibe, often paired with a casual or streetwear outfit.

Baggy Jeans A wider, oversized fit throughout, baggy jeans offer a relaxed, roomy silhouette, typically associated with 90s fashion or street style.

Cargo Jeans These jeans include additional pockets on the sides, offering a utilitarian and functional style. Often loose-fitting, cargo jeans provide both comfort and practicality.

Printed Jeans Jeans that feature patterns, graphics, or designs such as florals, stripes, or abstract prints, adding a bold, expressive element to your outfit.
