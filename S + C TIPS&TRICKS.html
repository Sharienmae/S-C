Tips and Tricks

Coquet Look - A playful and flirtatious style featuring soft, feminine clothing like dresses, skirts, and accessories that exude charm and elegance.

Casual - A relaxed and comfortable style focused on everyday wear, such as jeans, t-shirts, hoodies, and sneakers.

Baggy Look - A relaxed and oversized style characterized by loose-fitting clothing, such as baggy jeans and oversized t-shirts.

Manly Look - A masculine style that emphasizes structured clothing like tailored suits, denim jackets, and boots.

Summer Old Money - A refined, timeless style reflecting wealth and class, incorporating light fabrics like linen and tailored pieces.
