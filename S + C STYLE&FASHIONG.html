Trends

That refers to the general direction or pattern of change in style, behavior, or preferences over time. In fashion, trends represent popular styles, colors, or designs that gain widespread attention and influence how people dress or accessorize during a specific period.

Y2K

Inspired by the late '90s and early 2000s, this trend features bold colors, low-rise jeans, graphic tees, crop tops, and chunky sneakers, bringing back a nostalgic vibe with futuristic twists.

Street Wear

A blend of urban style and high fashion, streetwear includes oversized hoodies, graphic tees, baggy pants, sneakers, and accessories like baseball caps and chunky chains, all focused on comfort, individuality, and a cool, effortless look.

Old Money

A sophisticated and timeless style characterized by understated luxury, tailored suits, cashmere sweaters, blazers, pleated trousers, and classic accessories like leather handbags and loafers, often in neutral tones and premium fabrics.
