Trends

Trends refer to the general direction or pattern of change in style, behavior, or preferences over time. In fashion, trends represent popular styles, colors, or designs that gain widespread attention.

Y2K

Inspired by the late '90s and early 2000s, this trend features bold colors, low-rise jeans, graphic tees, crop tops, and chunky sneakers.

Street Wear

A blend of urban style and high fashion, streetwear includes oversized hoodies, graphic tees, baggy pants, sneakers, and accessories like baseball caps.

Old Money

A sophisticated and timeless style characterized by understated luxury, tailored suits, cashmere sweaters, blazers, and classic accessories.
