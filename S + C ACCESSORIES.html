Accessories

A WatchA timepiece worn on the wrist, combining functionality and style. Watches can range from classic designs to more modern, digital styles.

Cap:A casual headwear accessory, often with a brim or visor, providing shade from the sun and adding a sporty or laid-back element to your outfit.

Earrings Jewelry worn on the ears, available in various styles such as studs, hoops, or dangly designs, offering a simple yet elegant way to accessorize.

Shades Stylish sunglasses that protect your eyes from the sun while adding a cool, fashionable touch to any outfit.

Scarf A versatile accessory worn around the neck or shoulders for warmth, comfort, or style, often made from wool, cotton, or silk.
